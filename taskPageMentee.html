Mentee Expectations

Customer Service

Hone your customer service skills by working with your mentor to assist and delight our valued customers. Learn to handle inquiries, offer product recommendations, and ensure an exceptional experience for every visitor to our establishment. Your mentor will guide you in creating memorable moments for our patrons, enhancing your ability to provide top-notch service.

Beverage Crafting

Join your mentor in crafting delightful new beverages that will tantalize the taste buds of our customers. In this task, you'll learn the art of creating unique and refreshing drinks, gaining insights into the world of coffee, tea, and more. From selecting premium ingredients to perfecting the art of brewing, you'll develop the skills needed to craft innovative and delicious drinks.

Food Preperation

Discover the culinary world as you collaborate with your mentor to create delectable food items. From the kitchen to the plate, you'll be introduced to the art of food preparation and presentation. Master the techniques of making mouthwatering dishes that satisfy our customers' cravings. Your mentor will share their expertise, helping you become a skilled artisan in the world of food.
