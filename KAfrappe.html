How to Make Delicious Frappes

Frappes are a popular and refreshing coffee-based drink that our customers love. Here's how to make a perfect frappe:

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of freshly brewed coffee
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 3 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 cups of ice
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Chocolate or caramel syrup (optional)

Instructions

  1. Start by brewing a cup of your favorite coffee. Let it cool down to room temperature.
  2. In a blender, add the cooled coffee, milk, sugar, and ice.
  3. Blend the mixture until it's smooth and has a slushy consistency.
  4. Pour the frappe into a tall glass.
  5. If desired, top it with whipped cream and drizzle with chocolate or caramel syrup.

Tips and Variations

Here are some tips and variations to enhance your frappe-making skills:

  • Use flavored syrups like vanilla or hazelnut for a different twist.
  • Add a scoop of your favorite ice cream for a creamier texture.
  • Adjust the sugar and milk to suit your taste preferences.
  • Experiment with different toppings like crushed cookies or sprinkles.

Serving

Serve your delicious frappe with a straw and enjoy! Be sure to make our customers' day with this refreshing drink.
